The History of Women's Day

  • 1956 – 20,000 women marched to the Union Buildings in Pretoria to protest pass laws.
  • 1994 – Women's Day declared a national holiday in democratic South Africa.
  • Today – We continue to celebrate women's achievements and fight for gender equality.
